I am a postdoc in the electrical engineering department at the University of Michigan working with Professor Jeffrey A. Fessler and Professor Jon-Fredrik Nielsen in the Functional MRI Lab on quantitative MRI analysis of the brain.  In June 2026 I completed my Ph.D. in the same lab. I specifically focus on studying myelin and white matter lesions of tissue samples from patients with Alzheimer’s Disease.  My research interests include both experimental and computational aspects of MRI imaging, including scanning, data acquisition, sequence development, image processing, image reconstruction, and quantitative parameter mapping.
